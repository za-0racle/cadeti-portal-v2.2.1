Our Core Mandates

Youth Development & Leadership

Nurturing visionary leaders through disciplined character building.

Community Service & National Development

Engaging in impactful projects for national progress.

Security Support & Public Safety

Strategic support for community policing and intelligence.

National Unity & Social Cohesion

Fostering brotherhood and peace across all boundaries.

The Vision

To be a world-class youth leadership organization, producing disciplined, visionary, and socially responsible Nigerians who lead with integrity and contribute meaningfully to national development.

The Mission

To develop a generation of disciplined and civic-minded youth leaders through structured, uniformed training, community service, leadership development, and strategic support to public safety.

Primary Objectives

  • Foster disciplined leadership.
  • Support community policing.
  • Equip for disaster response.
  • Promote entrepreneurship.
  • Tactical character training.
  • National unity coordination.
